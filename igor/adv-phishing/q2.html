Solution

This is a well-executed phishing attempt. Emails concerning the tracking of a package are a regular part of our lives, and this one has almost all the details right.
Dear client, Your package has been delivered to the local UPS office.
The tracking# is: 1Z12Y6169096771351 and can be used at:
http://wwwapps.upps.com/WebTracking
The shipping invoice can be downloaded from:

Solution

Always check links by hovering over the URL before clicking. In this case, only one of the two links leads to a malicious download on a site called cooplapara.com. This obviously does not belong to UPS.
