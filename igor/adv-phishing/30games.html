Phishing games

GAME

Spot The Phishing Scam

In this game-like course, we will present you with different emails and websites that contain highlighted items. We ask you to study those items carefully.

When you hover/click over each of them, you can either click "unsafe," if the item seems as an indicator of a phishing email, or you can click "safe," if the item appears to impose no risk.

After you’ve made a choice for an item, hover/click over it again to view the correct solution.
